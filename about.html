About Kome Parnell

Independent Creative Photographer

I am a photographer working with clients from New York to San Diego for over 10 years. I specialize in portrait and wedding photography with this collection of images being amongst some of my favorite that I hope you enjoy. I live with my boyfriend in our corner of San Diego with our 2 cats Kiko and Pedro and our Pomeranian Paco who swears he's a human. I have an absurd love of musicals since I was little so yes, Hamilton is high on that list (like crazy high), Wes Anderson movies and I can binge watch Bones, Grey's Anatomy or Fringe while editing and almost quote any scene without looking while editing..yeah even the end production credits. I am SO ver proud. I am a tea and scotch drinker (not at the same time... or am I?). I LOVE Photography. My next favorite is showing off my non existent dance moves but hey.. feeling free to express a moment of pure joy when you capturing great photos deserves it so let's experience it together :)
